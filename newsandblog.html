Planning, Partners and Products Enable Day-One Performance at Medical Center

Originally published in the September 2014 issue of Cabling Installation & Maintenance Magazine, this piece explores how the newly constructed MaineGeneral Medical Center achieved full operational readiness on opening day through strategic planning, the right partners, and carefully specified infrastructure products.

When a new hospital opens its doors, there is no grace period - clinical systems, communications, and network infrastructure must perform flawlessly from minute one. The MaineGeneral Medical Center project demonstrates how early collaboration between owners, consultants, contractors, and manufacturers can eliminate day-one risk.

The project required meticulous coordination of structured cabling, fiber backbone, and communications systems across a complex clinical environment. Products from FAS & Associates principals played a key role in meeting the project's demanding specifications.
